CCD, IIT Guwahati

Centre for Career Development handles all aspects of campus placements for the graduating students of all the departments. CCD is well equipped with excellent infrastructure to support every stage of the placement processes. CCD staff members assist in arranging Pre-Placement Talks, Written Tests, Group Discussions, and Interviews etc. as per the requirements of the recruiters.
